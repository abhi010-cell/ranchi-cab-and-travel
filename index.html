Explore Popular Destinations

Dassam Falls

A magnificent waterfall cascading from a height of 144 feet, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic beauty. Located about 40km from Ranchi, this pristine waterfall is perfect for nature lovers and photographers. The roaring sound of water falling from such a height creates a mesmerizing atmosphere.

Netarhat

Known as the 'Queen of Chotanagpur', Netarhat offers breathtaking sunrise views and pristine natural landscapes. At an altitude of 3,700 feet, it's famous for its spectacular sunrises and sunsets. The place is blessed with dense forests, beautiful waterfalls, and comfortable weather throughout the year.

Betla National Park

Home to diverse wildlife including tigers, elephants, and various bird species in their natural habitat. This national park offers exciting safari experiences and chances to spot rare wildlife. The park also houses historic structures including an old fort, adding a cultural dimension to your wildlife experience.

Patratu Valley

Patratu Valley is known for its lush green hills, winding roads, and breathtaking views. The serene waters of the dam and the tranquil surroundings make it ideal for photography, picnics, and nature exploration. It’s a perfect retreat for both adventure seekers and those looking for peace.
